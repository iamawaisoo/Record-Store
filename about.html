About Us

Record Store is more than just a music store – it’s a cultural landmark in the city. Since our inception in 1985, we’ve been committed to delivering a diverse range of music, hosting exciting live performances, and creating a space for music enthusiasts to connect.

Our store caters to everyone, from toddlers discovering nursery rhymes to seniors enjoying classic devotional melodies. We’re proud to house an extensive collection of CDs, tapes, vinyl, books, and magazines that span genres, generations, and geographies.

Our Journey

  • 1985: Record Store was founded to celebrate music in all its forms.
  • 1995: Expanded to host live contests and shows for music lovers.
  • 2005: Introduced membership programs with exclusive offers and discounts.
  • 2010: Launched the largest collection of regional and devotional music.
  • 2024: Launched RhythmHouse.com to reach a global audience of music lovers.
